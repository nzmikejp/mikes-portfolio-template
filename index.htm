About

Life is like a box of chocolates. Hahaha, Nah seriously though. Starting my career at 16, I was persuaded to become a signwriter. Blink for a minute and 25 years had passed. In the signage industry, I have found myself working at all levels and finding my way up the creative ladder for some pretty cool companies.

At the peak of my employment, I found myself managing a team of 25 employees. With reaching as far as I could in management, I decided to start my own graphic/signwriting business. Owning my business for 13 years, I can say I have seen, created and fabricated all weird and wonderful creations. I found myself being the can-do man to my longstanding client's design needs. While I was running my business, I was also regularly upskilling. Any creative software I could get my hands on, I would. This thurst leads me slowly to web design. I found designing websites quite satisfying; it ticked all the boxes.

In 2019 I sold my business and undertook further learning, in which I have just completed a diploma in UX Design/Web Development. If you would like to work with me, please feel free to reach out and make contact. We can do coffee.
